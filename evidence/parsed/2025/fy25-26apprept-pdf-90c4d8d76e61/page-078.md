Department of Labor and Employment
The Department of Labor and Employment is responsible for managing the State’s unemployment insurance
and family and medical leave insurance programs; providing services to employers and job seekers; enforcing
laws concerning labor standards and providing labor market information ; regulating the workers’
compensation industry ; ensuring public safety and consumer protections ; and administering vocational
rehabilitation services.
