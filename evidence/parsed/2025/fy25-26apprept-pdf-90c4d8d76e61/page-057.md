Department of Higher Education
The Department is responsible for higher education and vocational training programs in the state. The
Colorado Commission on Higher Education (CCHE) serves as the central policy and coordinating board for the
Department, which includes oversight of financial aid programs. This department includes the State Historical
Society (History Colorado), which manages museums, distributes grants, and supports historic preservation.
