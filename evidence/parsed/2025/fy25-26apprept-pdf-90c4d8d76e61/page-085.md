Legislative Department
The Legislative Branch includes the elected members of the House of Representatives and the Senate, and the
necessary staff to support these legislators. The staff include those employed by the House and the Senate,
and those employed by the following legislative service agencies:
• Office of the State Auditor;
• Joint Budget Committee Staff;
• Legislative Council Staff; and
• Office of Legislative Legal Services.
The legislative service agency staff are full-time nonpartisan professionals. The House and the Senate employ
both partisan and nonpartisan professional staff, and some of these staff serve part- time when the General
Assembly is in session.
