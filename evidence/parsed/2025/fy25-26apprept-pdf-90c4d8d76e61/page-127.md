Department of State
The Department of State (DOS) administers statutory provisions pertaining to elections; manages the
statewide voter registration database; implements the Help America Vote Act (HAVA); oversees campaign
finance reporting; registers lobbyists and monitors the filing of required disclosures; collects, maintains, and
provides public access to business filings; regulates cha rities and charitable gaming; and certifies notaries
public.
