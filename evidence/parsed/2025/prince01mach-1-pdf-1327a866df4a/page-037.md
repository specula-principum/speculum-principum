26 NICCOL0 machiavelli
abolished the old militia, raised a new one, aban-
doned his old friendships and formed others;
and as he had thus friends and soldiers of his
own choosing, he was able on thisfoundation to
build securely, so that while he had great trouble
in acquiring his position he had little in main-
taining it.
