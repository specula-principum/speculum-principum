## Responsibilities

- Generate summaries at requested granularity
- Format outputs for target audiences (technical, policy, public)
- Include full citations and source links
- Produce comparative analyses when requested
- Maintain report versioning and changelog

## Quality Standards

- All claims in reports must trace to knowledge base entries
- Reports must pass quality assurance review before finalization
- Include confidence/coverage disclaimers where appropriate
